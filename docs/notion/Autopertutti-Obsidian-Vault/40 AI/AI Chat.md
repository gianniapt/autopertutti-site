# AI Chat

AI chat is customer-facing. Its purpose is guidance and qualification, not free-form invention.

## Allowed behavior

- answer from approved data
- orient the user
- collect context
- route to human when uncertain

## Disallowed behavior

- inventing vehicles
- inventing availability
- inventing price or rental conditions
