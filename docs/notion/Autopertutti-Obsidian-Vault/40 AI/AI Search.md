# AI Search

AI search turns natural language into structured filters for the real database.

## Pattern

1. user writes a need
2. AI extracts structured intent
3. backend queries the database
4. real results are shown
5. user moves to [[20 Product/Vehicle Detail Page]] or [[40 AI/AI Search Lead Generation]]

## Why it matters

It complements normal filters in [[20 Product/Vehicle Catalog]] and unlocks demand intelligence for [[30 Systems/Analytics and Tracking]].
