# Internal AI

Internal AI helps the team work faster and cleaner.

## Main uses

- lead classification
- lead priority
- lead summary
- next best action
- CRM anomaly detection
- weekly reporting

## It depends on

- [[30 Systems/CRM and Lead Flow]]
- [[30 Systems/HubSpot as Memory]]
- [[30 Systems/Analytics and Tracking]]
- [[30 Systems/Data Quality]]

## It should not do

- make final business decisions
- change prices
- promise availability
