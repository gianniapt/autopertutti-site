# Business Context

Autopertutti operates as a multi-service automotive business. The business context connects [[10 Business/Company Profile]], [[10 Business/Locations]], [[10 Business/Commercial Offer]], and [[10 Business/Trust Signals]] to the digital platform.

## Why it matters

The new platform must support:

- car sales
- car rental
- motorcycle sales
- motorcycle rental
- workshop and maintenance
- car wash
- lead generation
- CRM operations
- AI-assisted support

## Strategic consequence

Because the business is multi-line and multi-location, the platform must be modeled around [[30 Systems/Database Model]], [[30 Systems/Vehicle Stock System]], [[30 Systems/CRM and Lead Flow]], and [[50 Execution/MVP Scope]].
