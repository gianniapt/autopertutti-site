# Data Quality

Data quality determines whether the website sells well, whether CRM works cleanly, and whether AI behaves safely.

## Quality checks

- vehicles without images
- vehicles without price
- outdated availability
- sold vehicles still published
- duplicate records
- incomplete FAQ
