# Vehicle Stock System

The stock system is the operational base behind the public catalog.

## It must support

- creating and editing vehicles
- media management
- publication states
- availability states
- location assignment
- export readiness

## Why it matters

Without a reliable stock system, [[20 Product/Vehicle Catalog]], [[20 Product/Vehicle Detail Page]], and [[40 AI/AI Search]] become unreliable.
