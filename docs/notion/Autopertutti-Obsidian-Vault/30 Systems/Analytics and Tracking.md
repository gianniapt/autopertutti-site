# Analytics and Tracking

Analytics closes the loop between product, lead capture, and business outcome.

## It should measure

- traffic by area
- catalog activity
- CTA clicks
- lead submissions
- WhatsApp clicks
- AI search usage
- conversion quality

## Why it matters

Without tracking, the team cannot validate [[50 Execution/MVP Scope]], measure [[40 AI/AI Search]], or prioritize [[50 Execution/Priorities and Next Steps]].
