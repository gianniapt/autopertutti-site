# Customer Journey

The customer journey is the path from discovery to contact to sale or booking.

## Main path

1. traffic source
2. catalog or landing page
3. vehicle or service discovery
4. CTA click
5. [[30 Systems/Lead Capture]]
6. [[30 Systems/CRM and Lead Flow]]
7. follow-up

## Journey variants

- purchase journey
- rental journey
- workshop journey
- AI-assisted search journey through [[40 AI/AI Search]]
