# Vehicle Catalog

The catalog should be split by intent:

- cars for sale
- cars for rent
- motorcycles for sale
- motorcycles for rent

This separation reduces confusion in [[20 Product/Customer Journey]] and improves targeting in [[30 Systems/Analytics and Tracking]].

## Catalog goals

- make discovery easy
- expose the right filters
- keep the cards clean
- push the user toward [[20 Product/Vehicle Detail Page]]
